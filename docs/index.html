Kailera breaks biotech IPO record with $625M debut

BioSpace · 2026-04-18 · ⏱ 3 min read

Kailera Therapeutics raised a record-breaking $625 million in the largest biotech IPO ever, surpassing Moderna's 2018 record of $600 million. The obesity-focused company trades under KLRA and closed up 62.5% on its first day, bringing its total funding to over $1.6 billion including previous Series A and B rounds. The company's lead asset ribupatide showed 12.1% weight loss in Phase 2 trials and is now advancing through three Phase 3 studies.
