TurboQuant: Redefining AI efficiency with extreme compression

Google Research · 2026-03-25

Google Research introduces TurboQuant, a compression algorithm that reduces LLM key-value cache memory by at least 6x and delivers up to 8x speedup with zero accuracy loss. The algorithm uses a two-stage approach combining PolarQuant and Quantized Johnson-Lindenstrauss transforms to compress AI model vectors to just 3 bits while maintaining mathematical precision.

💡 This breakthrough could democratize AI by enabling large models to run on consumer hardware and dramatically reduce infrastructure costs for AI deployment.

🤖 Slot 3

Arm launches AGI CPU, its first in-house chip targeting AI data centers

Arm Newsroom · 2026-03-25

Arm enters the silicon market for the first time in its 35-year history with the AGI CPU, a 136-core processor designed for AI data centers. Developed with Meta as lead partner, the chip claims 2x performance per rack versus x86 systems and targets agentic AI workloads.

💡 This represents a fundamental business model shift for Arm from IP licensing to direct hardware competition, potentially disrupting the data center CPU market dominated by Intel and AMD.
