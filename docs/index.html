Cytokinetics heart drug scores in closely watched trial

BioPharma Dive · 2026-05-05 · ⏱ 3 min read

Cytokinetics' Myqorzo succeeded in Phase 3 trials for non-obstructive hypertrophic cardiomyopathy, hitting dual primary endpoints with statistically significant improvements in peak oxygen consumption and heart health scores. This differentiates it from Bristol Myers Squibb's Camzyos, which failed in the same indication last year, potentially opening access to the one-third of HCM patients with non-obstructive disease. The drug showed manageable safety profile though 27 patients experienced drops in ejection fraction versus one on placebo.

💡 Success in both obstructive and non-obstructive HCM could expand Cytokinetics' addressable market to over $5 billion annually while establishing competitive advantage over Bristol Myers' $1B+ Camzyos franchise.
