CNBC / STAT News · 2026-03-20

🌀 Slot 4

arXiv declares independence from Cornell after 35 years, seeks $300K CEO amid funding pressures

Science Magazine · 2026-03-20

The pioneering preprint server arXiv will become an independent nonprofit on July 1, ending its 23-year relationship with Cornell University. The move aims to diversify funding sources for its $6.7M annual operation amid explosive growth to 300,000 submissions annually and challenges from AI-generated 'slop' submissions.

💡 As the backbone of scientific publishing for physics, math, and computer science, arXiv's independence could reshape how open science infrastructure is funded and governed, with implications for AI research dissemination and academic publishing models.
