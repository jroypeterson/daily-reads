Trump declares 100% pharma tariffs—with exemptions

BioPharma Dive · 2026-04-04

President Trump imposed 100% tariffs on patented pharmaceutical products and ingredients under Section 232, taking effect in 120 days for large companies and 180 days for smaller ones. Analysts view this as removing policy overhang since most major drugmakers already cooperated, making the "overall threat to the sector low."
